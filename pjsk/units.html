The Five Units

Leo/need

This is a group about nostalgia, friendship, and hardship featuring Ichika Hoshino as the main character, along with Saki Tenma, Honami Mochizuki, and Shiho Hinomori. Fun fact, this group includes two sister characters, Saki being Tsukasa's sister, and Shiho being Shizuku's sister.


MORE MORE JUMP!

This group has a idol theme, with a big focus on how the industry affects its idols and their friendships with eachother. The main character is Minori Hanasato, along with her new found friends Haruka Kiritani, Airi Momoi, and Shizuku Hinomori.


Vivid BAD SQUAD

Vivid BAD SQUAD is a hip-hop based group, about finding self confidence and your own voice, featuring Kohane Azusawa, as the focus, and An Shiraishi, Akito Shinonome, and Toya Aoyagi as the other characters.


Wonderlands x Showtime

Wonderlands x Showtime, the theatrical facet of this metaphorical pentagon. Featuring the talents of Tsukasa Tenma as the lead, and Emu Otori, Nene Kusanagi, and Rui Kamishiro, as the supporting characters.


25ji, Nightcord de.

25ji, Nightcord de. the emo group of the game. Even with my limited knowledge of the other group's backstories, I can confidently say that 25ji probably has some of the most depressing characters in the game, Kanade Yoisaki, top dog emo, and her comrades, Asahina Mafuyu, Ena Shinonome, and Akiyama Mizuki.
