Crash Course: Project Sekai/Hatsune Miku: Colorful Stage 101

If you're here, you probably want to get better at Project Sekai, or just want to know what it is.

First of all, as an introduction, Project Sekai is a videogame made by Crypton, the company that made Vocaloid (more on that later), and Colorful Palette, a child studio of Craft Egg (who made another Japanese rhythm game by the name of "Girls Band Party!" or "Bandori")

Vocaloid is a voice synthesis program where the user can make a selection of voices (most of which are derived from voice providers) sing or say whatever they please. Coming in a variety of languages, people all around the world love Vocaloid music.

So, what is Project Sekai? It's a story, gacha, and rhythm game, all at once, along with virtual concerts being held regularly.
